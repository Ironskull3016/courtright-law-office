About Me

Crystal Courtright

Criminal Defense Attorney

Crystal Courtright is a criminal defense attorney serving clients throughout Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Chesapeake, Hampton, and Portsmouth.

After graduating from Regent University, Crystal built her criminal defense experience in both public service and private practice. She has represented people as a public defender and has also practiced with established law firms in Virginia Beach and Norfolk, developing years of courtroom and trial experience.

Crystal is especially passionate about difficult cases that demand careful legal research and unconventional thinking. She believes strong defense work requires curiosity: a willingness to question assumptions, examine the evidence closely, and look at a problem from angles that others may have overlooked.

The Fourth Amendment is a particular area of passion in her work. Constitutional protections against unreasonable searches and seizures are not technicalities — they are fundamental rights, and they deserve to be defended.

Outside the law, Crystal has a strong artistic side. She has practiced woodburning and pyrography for more than a decade, an art form that reflects the same patience, attention to detail, and creative perspective she brings to complex legal problems.
